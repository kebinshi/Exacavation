Exacavator Minecraft

Welcome to Excavator Minecraft.

This is the wikipedia for Excavator Minecraft server. Excavator Minecraft is a server entrenched in pre-historic times. You have been born as a slave and your goal is to work your way up the life classes.

Custom Items

Bio-Fossils

Bio-Fossils are dropped when excavating a mining zone. Different blocks have different tendencies of dropping Bio-Fossils. Bio-Fossils can be sold in the Black Market for extra gold.

Bio-Fossils Drop Rate is affected by enchantment "Luck".

Sand (id: 12): 10% Bio-Fossil Drop Rate 
Red Sand (id: 12:1): 15% Bio-Fossil Drop Rate

Custom Lores

On certain items, there will be custom lores to facilitate the story line of the server. These lores does not affect the damage values of the item and hence does not affect the gameplay.

Custom Enchantments

Luck

Luck is an enchantment derived from Looting in vanilla Minecraft. When a player uses a tool with the enchantment "Luck", the drop rates for items are increased by percentages (e.g 5% increase).

Highest level of enchantment "Luck" is 3.
